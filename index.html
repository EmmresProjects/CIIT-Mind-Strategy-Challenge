Tic Tac Toe
You play as X. Click an empty cell on the 3x3 grid to place your X.

The AI plays as O and will automatically respond after your move, trying to block you or win.

Aim to get three X's in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before the AI does.

If the grid fills without a winner, it's a draw. To restart, press the 'Restart' button.

After finishing or if you want to count the round without completing, press 'Mark Complete'.

Pattern Memory
Press 'Start' to begin. Watch the sequence of colored pads flashing (red, green, blue, or yellow).

After the sequence ends, repeat it by clicking the pads in the same order. Each correct repetition adds a new color to the sequence, increasing the level.

If you make a mistake, the level restarts but your best score is tracked.

Reach level 5 for a strong result and to count the round.

Press 'Mark Complete' if you want to count without reaching level 5.

UNO
Select number of AI players (1-10) from dropdown, then press 'New Match' to deal 7 cards each and start.

Match the top discard pile card by color or number/symbol.

Click a card in your hand to play it if it matches. If no match, click the 'Deck' to draw one card (ends your turn).

Action cards:
S skips the next player's turn.
R reverses the play direction (clockwise to counterclockwise or vice versa).

Play all your cards first to win. AI plays automatically on their turns, choosing the first playable card or drawing if none.

Press 'Mark Complete' after win or to count round early.
